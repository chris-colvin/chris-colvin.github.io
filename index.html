About me

I am a Reader in Economic History in the Department of Economics at Queen’s Business School, Queen’s University Belfast, and Co-Director of the university’s Centre for Economic History (QUCEH).

I also serve as Associate Director for Outreach and Engagement at the Centre for Economics, Policy and History, a Government of Ireland-funded Centre of Excellence linking Queen’s with Trinity College Dublin.

As an Associate Editor at Business History, I have particular responsibility for submissions that employ quantitative methods.

Aside from my research, I conduct pedagogical scholarship on ways to better integrate economic history into the economics curriculum. My edited book, An Economist’s Guide to Economic History, provides accessible historical material that can be incorporated into undergraduate teaching.
