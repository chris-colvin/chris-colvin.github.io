Requesting an academic reference

I will write academic references for current and recent undergraduate students when:

  • I know who you are (at minimum, I took you for tutorials).
  • You provide an electronic transcript of your module grades to date.
  • You explain the purpose of the reference and why you want me to act as referee.
  • You specify where the reference should be sent and the deadline.
