Supervision philosophy

Economic history needs to be reinserted into the research and teaching agendas of economics departments and business schools. If you share that conviction, I am keen to work with you.

The academic job market is tough. My goal is to ensure that students graduate with a collection of interesting papers addressing important questions, and with the skills and confidence needed to develop an independent research agenda.

I help my PhD students to develop a strong second field alongside economic history, and to think explicitly about how their historical research can contribute to wider debates in that field.
